Thriftin'

Definition of thrift shop : a shop that sells secondhand articles and especially clothes and is often run for charitable purposes.


This Al Yousef Center thrift store commercial was made for the 30 Minute Film Festival by a crew of four NYUAD students.
As the rise of fast fashion gains momentum, bringing not only overpriced items and massive environmental concerns with it, we decided to promote a much more sustainable and affordable solution: The Thrift Store!
